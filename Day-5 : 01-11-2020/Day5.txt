				Blinking of LED / Working with peripherals of BBB
Two Data Sheets
1. For BBB connections
2. For SOC 

Page 4057 in TRM for GPIO
	GPIO Pins - 32 for each module
	BBB has 4 module of GPIO
	32*4 = 128 pins of GPIO available in BBB 
	
	
Page70 in BBB_SRM for Expansion headers pin configuration
	In Mode7
	gpio_moduleNumber[Pin_number]
	Eg: gpio2[6]  - gpio module 2 pin 6
	    gpio1[3]  - gpio module 1 pin 3
	module numbers are - 0 1 2 3
Pgae15 in AM335x Sotata processors for soc pin configurarition
	18*6 =324 pins in soc
